Smart Ride: A bicycle navigation application

National entrepreneur start-up campaign, China
2014

This project had been proposed when I join the National entrepreneur start-up campaign. The idea is providing a safe and reliable navigation application. The shining point is that projecting laser parallel lines indicate the direction for the bicycle riders. A demo had been made for proof of the concept. This application had won the Bronze Award for the national campaign.
